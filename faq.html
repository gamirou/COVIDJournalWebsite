Learn how to use COVIDJournal

Account Profile Friends Posts Tests Symptoms Notifications Settings

It is very easy and straightforward to create an account with COVIDJournal. Once you have gone through the information pages when you first enter the app, press GET STARTED . If you are on the start up screen, just press REGISTER . The only method of sign up at the moment is by email.

Once you complete the last card, you will be brought to the signup page. This is where you have to fill in your details. We use these details to authenticate you (email and password). We use your birthday to verify your age. Once you've filled in your details and read through our Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions, tap the checkboxes and press REGISTER HERE . You can also add a profile picture by tapping the picture in the top right corner. You will receive an email verification link. Before you verify your account, you will not have all the features. You have to click the link in your email in order to unlock the features and then you can the make the most out of COVIDJournal.

From the start up screen press LOG IN. This will open a login page where you enter your email and password associated with your account. Once this is done, tap the LOG IN button and you will be redirected to your account.

It happens to the best of us. From the login page (LOG IN) tap FORGOT PASSWORD. You will be redirected to a page where you can enter the email address associated with your account. Once its been filled out, tap SEND VERIFICATION EMAIL. You should be sent an email with a link to reset your password.

Swipe to the right or press the navigation button (). Press the button (Account -> Log out). You will be directed to the startup screen.

It is sad to see you go, but it's your choice to leave. We have a feature under construction, but until then, you can send us an email at () delete@covidjournal.net. You will have to tell us why you want to delete your account. Your data will be erased from our servers once your account is deleted.

From your profile (Profile tab) simpily tap on your existing profile picture. If you haven't already allowed access to your camera, a popup will show asking you to allow it. If you have allowed access, a popup will show asking you to pick a new photo. This can either be from your camera, or your photos libaray.

At the moment you can only change your country and city. To edit them, press on your existing country and city, respectively. To save your changes, tap the button APPLY CHANGES. To edit your symptoms, tap the button CLICK HERE TO ENTER YOUR SYMPTOMS

There are multiple ways that you can find your friends. The most common way is through the friends page. Click the navigation button, or swipe to the right, and press FRIENDS. At the top you can see who sent you a friend request. Below, you can see a list of all your friends. You can also search your friend by pressing the button ADD USER on the navigation drawer. At the top of the screen will show a search bar. Type your friends name into the search bar and a list of people will be shown. From here you can view their profile image to verify that it is your friend.

Once you've located your friends its time to add them. You can send them a friend request by tapping the user button in the bottom right corner. Below their name, you can see if the request has been sent. They will have to accept this request before you are friends. If your friend has sent you a friend request you can accept it by going to the FRIENDS page as shown in the last card.

At the moment, you cannot view their friends in order to respect users' privacy.

At the moment, you cannot delete your friends. Sorry for the inconvience, we will add this feature in the next update.

Posts are the most important feature of the app. They are used to add contacts or places where you have been (still working on it). You will be notified immediately if you need to get a test. So make sure to keep it updated in order to make the most of COVIDJournal.

You can access the ADD PERSON page in two ways. You can either go to the HOME page and press the button underneath your countries' statistics, or you can swipe right and click ADD PERSON. Then, you can add a title to your post, the person's name. If you want to add a user, you will have to be friends with them first. If that user, does not have a COVIDJournal account, make sure to let them know yourself, or invite them to our app. You can also add at what time you met them. If you put an user down, the post will not be added immediately. The other person will be notified, and they will have to confirm whether it is true or not.

You can access the ADD ACTIVITY page in two ways. You can either go to the HOME page and press the button underneath your countries' statistics, or you can swipe right and click ADD ACTIVITY. Then, you can add a title to your post, a description, roughly how many people were there and when you did this activity. Examples include, going to the supermarket, or visiting a museum. You will not be notified based on these activities, but you can tell the authorities what you've been doing for the past 14 days, so they can investigate these places.

You can find your tests by going to the PROFILE page. You can find your tests below your city and country. You can see a list of your tests.

Click the button in the bottom right corner that says + ADD TEST. You will be redirected to a page that will let you add the date at which you took the test, the date at which you received the result and the result of the test. If your test is pending, you do not have to put your result. You can add it later.

You cannot see your friends' tests. It is confidential. You can only see if they are tested positive/unknown/pending and if you have been in contact with them.

You can delete a test by going to the PROFILE page and tap on the test you want to delete. Scroll down and press DELETE.

If you have received your result, look for the test by the result (it should say PENDING) or the date you took the test (Order: ). You can add the result and the date of the result. Once you are finished, press SAVE. Be careful, you cannot change the date at which you took the test. You will have to delete it and add a new test.

If your test is positive, all of the users that you were in contact in the last 14 days will be notified. If the result is pending or unknown, they are told to get a test themselves. If the result is negative and you were previously notified from other contacts, they will get a notification with the test of your result.

If a user is spamming you, send us an email at hello@covidjournal.net, and we will terminate their account. If needed, we can take legal action against them.

Similarly to tests, to access the SYMPTOMS page, you will need to go to the PROFILE page. To edit your symptoms, tap the button CLICK HERE TO ENTER YOUR SYMPTOMS. On the first page, you can edit the most common symptoms. On the second page, you can edit the more severe symptoms, (we hope you do not have them). If you have any of them, you will need medical assistance. On the last page, you can enter where you are right now, either at home or in hospital. If you have any symptoms, your contacts will be notified.

1. When someone sends you a friend request

2. When someone accepts your friend request

3. When a contact has or might have the coronavirus

4. When someone tries to add a post with your name

More events are being added soon.

You can also access your notifications by tapping the notification button () at the top of the screen. You can see the notifications from the notification bar. This is where you can accept/reject a post from your friends. This is also where you are reminded to add the result of a pending test. If you need to accept a friend request, you will be redirected to the Friends page.

You can delete notifications via the notifications tab by either tapping the cross on the individual notification, or by tapping Remove All to remove all notifications at once. In order to delete them from the Notifications page, you need to complete the actions needed. So, for example, if you are told to accept/reject a post, you are given two buttons to press. If you need to add the result of a pending test, you will be redirected to the user test page. If you complete the action, the notification will also be removed from the page.

Swipe left or click the navigation button and scroll down to the SETTINGS page.

You can turn notifications on or off, you can go to your profile, you can reset your password, verify your email or you can read our legal stuff.
